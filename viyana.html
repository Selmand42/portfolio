🇦🇹 Vienna Travel Plan

Vienna — 3 Days / Neubau Base

✈️ Arrival: 22 May 2026 (Friday) 10:00 — Vienna International Airport (VIE) 🏨 Stay: Myrthengasse 7, 07. Neubau, 1070 Vienna 🚇 Transport: Airport train/bus, metro, tram, walking 🎫 Tickets: Timed tickets recommended for Schönbrunn, Belvedere and the Natural History Museum; Sisi Museum/Hofburg interiors are optional
Day 1 — 22 May (Friday) Arrival, Neubau/MuseumsQuartier, Natural History Museum, Volksgarten/Burggarten, Donaukanal
Day 2 — 23 May (Saturday) Schönbrunn Palace, Naschmarkt, Innere Stadt, Ringstrasse, Rathaus, Karlskirche, Stadtpark
Day 3 — 24 May (Sunday) Belvedere, relaxed café time, 42 Vienna, Donaupark or Donauinsel, Prater / final dinner

📍 Base & Distance Notes — From Myrthengasse 7

Walkable core: MuseumsQuartier/Natural History Museum ~10–15 min walk, Rathaus ~15–20 min, Hofburg ~20 min, State Opera ~20–25 min.
By transit: St. Stephen’s Cathedral ~15–20 min, Schönbrunn ~25–35 min, Belvedere ~25–30 min, Prater ~25–35 min.
Evening returns: most central dinner areas are ~15–25 minutes from Myrthengasse by walking, tram or U-Bahn.

🎯 Vienna Must-Sees Included

This plan explicitly includes the key Vienna stops: Schönbrunn Palace, St. Stephen’s Cathedral, Hofburg Palace courtyards, Belvedere Palace, Vienna State Opera, Natural History Museum, 42 Vienna, Prater + Giant Ferris Wheel, Naschmarkt, Rathaus + Ringstrasse, Stadtpark, Volksgarten, Burggarten, Donaukanal, and a relaxed Donaupark / Donauinsel option.

🔄 Day 3 Alternatives

If Saturday gets too full: Move part of the inner-city route here and keep Donaupark/Donauinsel as a shorter sunset option.
If the weather is bad: Spend more time inside Belvedere, choose a longer café break, or add Sisi Museum/Hofburg interiors as an indoor fallback.

📝 Practical Notes

  • Use the WienMobil or ÖBB app for real-time routing, especially for cross-city tram and U-Bahn connections.
  • Base address: Myrthengasse 7, 07. Neubau, 1070 Vienna; most central sights are walkable or 15–30 minutes by transit.
  • Schönbrunn Palace and Upper Belvedere are the main timed-ticket priorities; the Natural History Museum is now included instead of the Kunsthistorisches Museum.
  • 42 Vienna is placed on Sunday because its Muthgasse/Döbling location pairs better with the Donau side than with the first arrival day.
  • Naschmarkt stalls are best Friday/Saturday; Sunday is not a market-stall day, so the plan uses Saturday lunch.
  • Karlskirche is grouped with Ringstrasse/Rathaus instead of being separated into another day.
  • Nature and water stops are included: Stadtpark, Volksgarten, Burggarten, Donaukanal and Donaupark/Donauinsel.
